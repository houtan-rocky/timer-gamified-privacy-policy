# Privacy Policy for Timer Gamified

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

Timer Gamified ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our desktop application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Timer Gamified operates entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with external servers or third parties.

The following data is stored locally on your device:

1. **Timer Settings and Preferences:**
   - Timer presets and durations
   - Custom timer configurations
   - User interface preferences
   - Sound and notification settings
   - Celebration and visual effect preferences

2. **Usage Statistics (Optional):**
   - Win/loss records for timer sessions
   - Daily statistics
   - Historical activity data

3. **License Information (if applicable):**
   - License key (stored locally in app data directory)
   - License activation timestamp

4. **Custom Media Files (if uploaded):**
   - Custom images uploaded by you
   - Custom sound files uploaded by you
   - All media files are stored locally on your device

5. **Application Settings:**
   - Auto-message configurations
   - Zone settings (danger/critical thresholds)
   - End question preferences
   - Overlay window settings

## How We Use Your Information

All data collected by Timer Gamified is used solely for the following purposes:

- **App Functionality:** To provide timer features, track your progress, and maintain your preferences
- **Personalization:** To remember your settings and customizations between app sessions
- **Statistics:** To display your win/loss records and activity history (if you choose to use this feature)

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored exclusively on your local device in the application's data directory
- No data is transmitted over the internet
- No data is shared with third parties
- No data is sent to external servers or analytics services

## Third-Party Services

Timer Gamified does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app uses:

- **Tauri Framework:** For desktop application functionality (no data collection)
- **System Notifications:** For local notifications only (no data transmission)
- **File System Access:** For storing your data locally on your device

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your information:

- **Access:** You can access all your data through the app's interface
- **Deletion:** You can delete your data by uninstalling the app or manually deleting the app's data directory
- **Portability:** Your data is stored in standard formats and can be accessed directly from your device's file system

## Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you:
- Uninstall the application
- Manually delete the application's data directory
- Clear the application's local storage

## Children's Privacy

Timer Gamified does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since the app does not collect or transmit any personal information, it is safe for users of all ages.
